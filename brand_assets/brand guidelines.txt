# SeaBreeze Home Service LLC
Brand Guidelines

Version 1.0  
Location San Diego County, California  
Industry Exterior Home Maintenance Services

---

# 1. Brand Identity

SeaBreeze Home Service LLC is a premium but approachable exterior home service company serving San Diego homeowners.

Our brand represents

• Trust  
• Professionalism  
• Safety  
• Attention to detail  
• Family-run service  
• Coastal California lifestyle

We position ourselves as a higher-quality alternative to cheap contractors.

---

# 2. Brand Voice

Tone should always be

Friendly  
Professional  
Confident  
Clear  
Trustworthy  

Avoid sounding

• Cheap
• Salesy
• Aggressive
• Corporate
• Robotic

Good examples

Professional window cleaning trusted by San Diego homeowners.

Protect your home's appearance and value.

Safe, professional exterior maintenance.

---

# 3. Target Customer

Primary Audience

• Homeowners
• Age 35 – 70
• Higher-income neighborhoods
• Property managers
• Real estate professionals

Common motivations

• Protect property value
• Maintain curb appeal
• Avoid liability from DIY ladder work
• Keep homes looking beautiful

# 5. Typography

Primary Font

Sans-serif modern font

Recommended

Inter  
Poppins  
Montserrat

Usage

Headings Bold  
Body text Regular  

Avoid decorative fonts.

---

# 6. Logo Usage

Logo should appear

• Top left of website header
• Website footer
• Marketing materials
• Service vehicles
• Social media graphics

Maintain clear space around logo.

Never distort or stretch logo.

---

# 7. Imagery Style

Use images that show

• Real homes
• Technicians working safely
• Before and after transformations
• Clean windows
• Pressure washing results
• Happy homeowners
• San Diego coastal homes

Preferred image style

Bright  
Natural lighting  
High resolution  
Professional appearance  

Avoid

• Stock photos that look fake
• Low resolution photos
• Dark or cluttered images
