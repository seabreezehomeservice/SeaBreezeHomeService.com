About This Service

Professional Pressure Washing Throughout San Diego

San Diego's climate — salt air near the coast, dust inland, seasonal rains — leaves homes with significant buildup on hard surfaces. SeaBreeze uses commercial-grade pressure washing equipment calibrated for each surface type, removing algae, mold, tire marks, and years of grime without damaging concrete, wood, or masonry.

Frequently Asked Questions

Concrete driveways, concrete patios, pavers, block walls, vinyl fencing, wood decks, stucco exteriors, and most hard exterior surfaces can be safely pressure washed. Delicate surfaces like painted wood siding benefit from soft washing instead.

San Diego driveways typically benefit from pressure washing once or twice per year, depending on tree cover, oil drips, and foot traffic. Coastal homes may benefit from more frequent washing due to salt air residue.

When done correctly at the right PSI and nozzle distance, pressure washing is safe for concrete. SeaBreeze uses appropriate pressure settings for each surface type. We do not use high-pressure settings on softer materials.

Yes. We service HOA common areas, apartment complexes, and commercial properties throughout San Diego County. Contact us for volume pricing on multi-unit properties.

Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to physically blast away dirt from hard surfaces. Soft washing uses low pressure with cleaning solutions to treat organic growth on delicate surfaces like roofs and painted siding. SeaBreeze offers both.
