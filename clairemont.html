About Clairemont

Professional Home Services in Clairemont

Clairemont is one of San Diego's large, established mid-city neighborhoods with a wide range of single-family homes, many built in the 1950s–1970s. The area's canyon-adjacent properties accumulate dust and debris, and many homes are now being updated with solar installations. SeaBreeze services Clairemont and neighboring communities including Linda Vista, Bay Park, and Kearny Mesa.
